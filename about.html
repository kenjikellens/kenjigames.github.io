Our Story

KenjiGames is an independent software and content studio founded by Kenji Kellens. Our core philosophy centers on a refusal of unnecessary framework bloat, prioritizing direct, high-performance web engineering and native app deployment.

From our flagship multi-platform media players like IVIDS and IVIDS Music, to our Gemini-powered cataloging solutions and gaming utilities, every project we undertake is built with an edge-to-edge cinematic design standard and smooth, low-latency execution.

Zero-Framework Standard

We leverage modern, native web APIs (ES Modules, custom components, and CSS grids) to keep bundles tiny and performance at a locked 60 FPS.

Our Core Pillars

The structural tenets guiding our creative and technical processes.

Performance First

Sluggish load times and laggy interfaces are unacceptable. We optimize every asset and pipeline step, targeting native speeds on TV web layers, mobile viewports, and desktop rigs alike.

Cinematic UI

Software should look and feel premium. We integrate responsive dark themes, ambient glowing gradients, subtle micro-animations, and balanced typography to deliver a premium user experience.

Open Source

We believe in giving back to the community. Most of our software packages, media systems, and helper scripts are completely open source and available for scrutiny and enhancement on GitHub.

Our Development DNA

We design, build, and deploy projects utilizing a stack tailored for efficiency, rapid rendering, and compatibility.

  • HTML5 & Semantic Structure
  • Vanilla CSS3 (Custom Variables)
  • Modern JavaScript (ES6+ Module Standard)
  • Android SDK & Kotlin Wrappers
  • Gemini AI API & Intelligent Automation
  • Responsive Grid & Flexbox Architectures
